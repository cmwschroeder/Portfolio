Pethub

Pethub is a web app for anyone interested in responsibly adopting a pet. On our webapp you can find pets in all areas to adopt. Even if you do not want to make the committment yet in adopting an animal, but still want to contribute to animals in need, we have a charity page that brings you to charities in all locations. If you are unsure of where to donate, there is a recommended list of charities available and much more.

Weather Dashboard

This webpage is a weather dashboard application. Just search for your city and it'll display the current weather and the next 5 day forecast. It also keeps track of your previous searches so don't worry about having to type the search in again, just click on the button with that cities name. If you want to clear your previous searches just click the red button to clear your searches and start anew.
